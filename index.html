Ti Wang (王体)

I'm currently a third-year master student in the School of Computer Science, Peking University, China, under the supervision of Prof. Hong Liu. I am a visiting Student at the Multimedia and Human Understanding Group (MHUG), University of Trento, Italy, supervised by Prof. Nicu Sebe.

My current research interest focus on 3D human pose and shape estimation in images/videos using deep learning and optimization approaches.
